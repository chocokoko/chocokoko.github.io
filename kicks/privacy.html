Privacy Policy for Baby Kicks

Last updated: April 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

a. Data You Provide

Baby Kicks stores your data locally on your device, including pregnancy-related notes such as kick counts and app preferences.

b. Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. We may receive limited purchase status information to enable app features.

c. Optional Cloud Sync (Future Feature)

Future versions may offer optional cloud synchronization. This feature will always be opt-in.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide app functionality
  • Enable in-app purchases
  • Improve user experience

We do not sell your data.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store your data on remote servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync (if available in future versions)
  • Your data is retained on your device until you delete it

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data except:

  • With Google Play for payment processing
  • When required by law

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws (such as the General Data Protection Regulation - GDPR), including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to delete your data
  • The right to restrict or object to processing

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete your data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings

If cloud sync is enabled in future versions, additional data management options will be provided within the app.

8. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:

  • Your consent (when you use the app)
  • The necessity to provide app functionality

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected by updating the date above.
